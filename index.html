Power Ranking Formula

A weighted weekly performance score blending two factors:

🏆 Win % (60% weight)
Based on your win/loss record over the last 4 weeks. Winning recently matters more than early season results.

📊 Points Scored (40% weight)
Your average points over the last 4 weeks relative to the league average. Scoring big even in a loss improves your ranking.

FINAL SCORE = (Recent Win % × 0.6 + Relative Scoring × 0.4) × 100

A score of 100 means you won all 4 recent games AND scored at the league average or above.
